About The Institute

About College

Priyadarshini Bhagwati College of Engineering (PBCOE), Nagpur, is an Autonomous Institute affiliated to RTM Nagpur University and approved by AICTE. With NAAC "A" accreditation, PBCOE is committed to academic excellence, innovation, and industry-aligned learning.

Department

The Civil Engineering Department was established in 2010 with an intake of 120 students. Guided by dedicated faculty, the department upholds high academic standards. Alumni serve in top positions across government, private companies, and research organizations.

ICI FEST 2026

A grand technical celebration dedicated to innovation in concrete technology. Organized under the ICI Student Chapter, this fest aims to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry practices, featuring competitions, lectures, and workshops.

Objectives

  • Promote technical knowledge and excellence in concrete technology and civil engineering.
  • Bridge the gap between academic learning and industry practices through expert interactions.
  • Encourage innovation and research-oriented thinking in sustainable technologies.
  • Provide a platform for skill development through competitions, workshops, and technical presentations.
  • Strengthen professional networking among students, academicians, and industry professionals.
  • Develop leadership, teamwork, and organizational skills among students.
